## Description (4000 chars max)

Learn any language from real YouTube videos — not textbooks, not flashcards, real content you actually want to watch.

BreakLingo turns any YouTube video into a complete language lesson. Paste a URL, and AI analyzes the entire transcript to extract vocabulary, grammar patterns, and practice material — all in seconds.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Find a Video — Search YouTube or paste a URL of any video in your target language
2. AI Analyzes — Our AI extracts vocabulary (with readings and definitions), grammar patterns, and generates practice sentences from the full transcript
3. Study — Review vocabulary cards with native pronunciation, study grammar rules with real examples from the video, and follow along with the interactive script
4. Quiz Yourself — 8 different question types test your knowledge: multiple choice, translation, fill-in-the-blank, listening comprehension, word arrangement, matching pairs, pronunciation, and multi-select
5. Practice Speaking — Have a voice conversation with AI about the video content. The AI speaks in your target language and gives real-time feedback on your responses

KEY FEATURES

• Learn from Real Content — YouTube videos in Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Spanish, French, German, and 50+ languages
• AI-Powered Analysis — Comprehensive vocabulary and grammar extraction with difficulty levels, readings, and part of speech
• Voice Conversation — Practice speaking with AI. Native silence detection auto-stops when you finish talking
• 8 Quiz Types — Multiple choice, translation, fill-blank, listening, word arrange, match pairs, pronunciation, and multi-select
• Conversation Summaries — After each speaking session, get detailed feedback on vocabulary usage, grammar patterns, and overall fluency
• Learning Path — Progressive units that unlock as you master each lesson, with star ratings and streak tracking
• Daily Streaks & XP — Stay motivated with daily streak tracking and an XP leveling system
• Personalized Recommendations — Video suggestions based on your study history and interests
• Offline Vocabulary — Study your saved vocabulary and grammar anytime

LANGUAGE SUPPORT

Japanese (with hiragana readings), Chinese (with pinyin), Korean, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Arabic, Hindi, and any language with YouTube captions.

POWERED BY AI

BreakLingo uses advanced AI to analyze video transcripts sentence by sentence, generating comprehensive vocabulary lists with readings, definitions, difficulty levels, and part of speech — far more thorough than manual study.
